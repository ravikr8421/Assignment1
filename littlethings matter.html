Little Things Matter----- Observe

Exam Discipline

You may have heard these things many times from many people. However, one can't speak of exam and not mention these little things that matter.

Organize yourself before the exam. To begin with, pack your exam essential the previous evening . These include statinary, and your hall ticket and identity card among othe things.

Acquainting yourself with the exam centre will help you get rid of last-minute jitters before exam .Make it a point to arrive early. A later or delayed entry creates avoidable panic, which will affect you adversly.

Follow all the rules with the exam centre; take only permitted items inside the examination room.

Don't be in a hurry to start writing the answer. Take a minute ot two to read the instruction and question paper properly.

On coming back home after the exam, make sure your hall ticket and other exam essentials are kept safely, as they will be needed again.

Exam times are nothing out of the ordinary. Stick to your normal routine, your everyday stationary---including something as minute as your regular pen---and comfortable clothes. Sticking to normality helps calm one's mind and averts unexpected surprises.
